The SCALA project emerged in 2009 with the proposal to support the literacy of children with autism. It is an online platform, available at scala.ufrgs.br, which seeks to support the person who does not practice orality. The site, also available in English and Spanish, has one version for the internet and another in the form of a tablet application. SCALA is also being used in the intellectual disability of people with cerebral palsy and with children who have selective mutism. The software can be used in the clinic, at school and even at home.
